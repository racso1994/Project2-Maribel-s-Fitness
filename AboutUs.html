ABOUT US

Maribel's Fitness

Maribel's Fitness was founded in 2020 as a family owned and operated business specialising in high-quality fitness services at great prices. Instead of being just another gym, our founder Maribel wanted to provide the best service in fitness exercies and set her mind to doing so! Over the last two years Maribel's Fitness has grown into one of Ogden's largest Zumba and fitness community, helping women and men live longer, happier and healthier lives.
